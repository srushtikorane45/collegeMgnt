FROM DEAN ACADEMICS DESK

Dr. Abhijit Chitre
B.E, M.E., Ph. D (Electronics and Communication Engg)
Dean Academics.

I pleasantly welcome you to Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology (VIIT). VIIT attained the prestigious autonomous
status from the year 2017-18. VIIT offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in Faculty of Engineering. VIIT
has adopted the choice-based credit system. VIIT regularly modernizes curricula with a higher standard to attain all the
graduate attributes such as teamwork, communication, ethics, financial and managerial skills etc. along with technical
competence. The institute also has the freedom to evolve methods of assessment of students’ performance, the conduct of
examinations and notification of results.

VIIT has adopted the philosophy of outcome-based education. At VIIT, we strive to forge the young students first as competent
engineers and then imbibe the present-day necessity of technical and life skills into them, whereby they are fully equipped to
take up the challenges of the industry. Our philosophy is to shape good and competent engineers. VIIT students are now being
placed not only in industries of repute within the country but also globally with consistently high pay packages. As a result of
the institute establishing a strong foothold in engineering education, VIIT is accredited with grade ‘A’ by NAAC and all
undergraduate programs have been granted NBA accreditation. VIIT has also obtained ISO certification.

At VIIT, ample opportunities, for internships in industry/research institute, industry-sponsored projects and collaborative
projects with research institutes like TIFR, IISER, IUCAA, etc. are available. As a result of exposure to research experience, many
of the students go abroad for higher studies. The interaction with industry and research institutes has helped to inculcate
analytical abilities among the students. The academic and research culture at VIIT helps the students to excel in University
examinations and placements. Several students from various programs of the institute have topped the rankers list of SPPU
every year.

As COVID-19 continues spreading in many countries of the world, VIIT has decided to implement an online education to over
3500 students from their homes, strong academic system, facilitated by qualified faculty, supported by ICT and state-of-the-art
infrastructure, make the education process an enjoyable journey for students.

Welcome to VIIT!
