Competative Exam Cell

VIIT established a new initiative known as COMPETITIVE EXAM CELL (CEC-VIIT) for all the students aspiring for competitive
exams. CEC is exclusively a student activity organized to create awareness about competitive exams held throughout India.

Till date, CEC has organized numerous sessions, with overwhelming response, setting CEC off on a high note. Officers from
civil services/military conducted very useful and motivational sessions.

IPS Prateek Thube conducted session to give idea about various facets of civil services. A very interesting session on
"MISSION UPSC" was conducted by Dr. Anurag Singh, an ex- sales tax commissioner.
