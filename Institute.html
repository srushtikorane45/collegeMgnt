Institute Innovaation Council


About IIc:

IIC, VIIT was established in the academic year 2018-2019 by a group of highly enthusiastic professors willing to promote
Entrepreneurship and Innovation in the college.

University-based clubs should work with school officials or a faculty adviser to find students who might be interested in
joining a IIC Club.


Vision:

To create a vibrant local innovation ecosystem and Establish Functioning Ecosystem for Scouting Ideas and Pre-incubation
of Ideas.

Mission:

  • Inculcating Innovation environment in Students and Faculty
  • Supporting Start-up
  • Achieving Atal Ranking of Institutions
  • Establishing Innovation eco system with product design
  • At least five IPRs generation
