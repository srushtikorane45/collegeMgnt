The great city of Pune has a rich cultural heritage that goes back to the seventeenth century. It is considered as the
source and inspirational centre for education from the very ancient times. Pune is now known as the Harvard, Stanford
and Michigan of this part of the world. With its round- the- year salubrious climate, it has attracted many an industry
including automobile, general engineering, chemicals and recently the IT. Pune boasts to be the city with scores of
institutes imparting quality education to the young generation for providing the necessary manpower to these diversified
enterprises.

The present need of the industry is challenging in that engineers must possess a combination of sound technical
background and IT skills. At the Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, we strive to forge the young students
first as competent engineers and then imbibe the present-day necessity of IT skills into them, whereby they are fully
equipped to take up the challenges of the industry.

Our philosophy is to shape up good and useful engineers and not just graduates. Our students are made to face the
industry head-on by way of numerous industrial projects which expose them to the state-of-the-art technology. We
boast the highest on-campus recruitment and the vast majority of our graduates are accepted by reputed American
universities for higher studies. We have tie-ups with various industries and educational institutions for transfer of
technology. It is therefore not surprising that we get students from all parts of India. We never cease to cope up with the
changing needs of the industry and have started postgraduate programs.

Our principal asset is the highly qualified faculty members, the majority of them with international exposure and our fully
equipped laboratories. Students and guardians are encouraged to have continuous interaction with the faculty for a
seamless understanding of the students progress. We give a keen emphasis on individual student counselling.
We are in the process of being one of the best engineering and IT institutions of India and are continuously putting
efforts to be the very best to provide quality education and placement at amicable cost.


Profile Of Director

Dr Vivek S. Deshpande, holds Bachelors and Masters of Engineering in Electronics & Telecommunications from
Savitribai Phule Pune University (earlier known as University of Pune) and Doctorate from Rashtrasant Tukadoji
Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. He has got 31 years of experience comprising of both industry
and academia. Dr Vivek S. Deshpande has worked at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune as Professor and
Head of Computer Engineering Department. Currently, his research interest includes Wireless Sensor Networks,
embedded systems, IoT, Industry 4.0 and High-Performance Computer Networks. Dr Vivek S. Deshpande has
completed his Post-Doctoral Research Fellowship from Technical University of Sofia, Bulgaria, Europe. He has
published more than 70 papers in International journals and conferences of repute and has filed 24 Indian patents. Dr
Vivek S. Deshpande is working on many research projects related to more social and industrial problems. Dr Vivek S
Deshpande is Senior member of IEEE, Pune Section (R10, India). He chairs Education Society and Industry Relations
for IEEE Pune Chapter. He is also a member of the conference committee of IEEE Pune Chapter.

Dr Vivek S. Deshpande has recently developed a Wireless Network Lab. This lab is now converted into Center of
Excellence for IoT. There are three verticals namely e-Health, Precision Agriculture and Smart City are targeted in this
COE. Dr. Vivek S. Deshpande is also an active member of the IP Lab, where industrial automation is complemented
with artificial intelligence and machine learning technology.
